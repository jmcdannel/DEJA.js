# Settings & Account

The Settings screen provides access to your account and layout configuration.

## Account Preferences

Manage your DEJA Cloud account details:

- **Display name** — How your name appears across DEJA.js apps
- **Email** — Your account email address
- **Theme** — Switch between light, dark, or system-default appearance

## Environment Configuration

View your layout's environment configuration — the same credentials used by the DEJA Server. This is helpful if you need to reconfigure the server or verify that your settings match.

Your configuration is available under **Settings > View Local Environment Configuration** and includes your layout ID, Firebase project details, and communication settings.

## Subscription Management

View your current subscription plan and manage your billing:

- **Hobbyist** — Free tier with basic features
- **Engineer** — Advanced features for serious operators
- **Conductor** — Full access for club-level operations

Visit the [Upgrade page](/docs/cloud/upgrade) for plan comparison and pricing details.
