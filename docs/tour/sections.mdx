# Exploring Layout Sections

The Sections screen presents your layout as a collection of themed areas. Each section represents a distinct part of the model railroad — a town, a mountain pass, a rail yard — with its own character and story.

## Section Cards

Each area appears as a card with:

- **Thumbnail image** with a play overlay on hover for quick media preview
- **Section name** and a short description
- **Category chips** (Intro, Technical) indicating available content types
- **View Details** button to open the full [area detail page](/docs/tour/area-detail)
- **Play button** to preview the section's intro video in a popup

## Default Sections

A typical layout includes sections like:

- **Tamarack Station** — Main passenger station with platforms, signals, and station activities
- **Thunder City** — Urban district with city sounds, street lights, and animated signs
- **Eagle Nest & Payette Canyon** — High mountain vistas and canyon operations with scenic trestles
- **Payette Subdivision** — Rural mainline with crossings, bridges, and long scenic runs
- **Roseberry Yard** — Busy freight yard and switching operations

## Navigating

Tap any section card to open its detail page, where you'll find the full media collection, construction details, and interactive effects specific to that area.
