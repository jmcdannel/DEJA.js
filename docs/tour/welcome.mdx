# Welcome Screen

The Welcome screen is the first thing visitors see when they start the Tour experience. It introduces your layout and provides multiple paths to begin exploring.

## Introduction Video

The left side of the screen features an embedded video player with your layout's overview video. This sets the stage for what visitors will discover — the different areas, interactive effects, and the technology behind the layout.

Below the video, a feature summary highlights what visitors can do:

- 🎮 Control interactive effects (lights, sounds, animations)
- 🗺️ Explore layout areas with videos and explanations
- 🔧 Technical deep dives into electronics and automation
- 🎧 Audio commentary on construction techniques

## Area Selection

The right side presents a grid of cards for each layout area. Tap any area to jump directly to its [detail page](/docs/tour/area-detail). Areas are organized by location on the layout — for example:

- Tamarack Station (marked as Recommended for first-time visitors)
- Roseberry Yard
- Payette Subdivision
- Thunder City
- Eagle Nest

## Layout Statistics

At the bottom of the welcome screen, key numbers about the physical layout:

- **Scale** — HO (1:87)
- **Track Length** — Total track footage
- **Interactive Effects** — Number of guest-accessible effects
- **Layout Areas** — Number of sections to explore
