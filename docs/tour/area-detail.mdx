# Area Detail

The Area Detail page provides an immersive look at a single section of your layout. It combines media, construction information, and interactive controls in a two-column layout.

## Left Column — Featured Content

### Featured Media

A large media player at the top showcases the area's featured video or audio content. Press play to watch an introduction to the area, a construction timelapse, or a technical deep-dive.

### Construction Details

Below the media player, a two-column panel displays:

- **Physical specs** — Platform length, building count, track length, and other construction details
- **Technical features** — Chips highlighting capabilities like Working Signals, DCC Block Detection, Scenic Lighting, and Sound Effects

### Interactive Effects

A grid of effect cards for effects specific to this area. Guests can activate lighting, sound, and animation effects directly from the area page — just like on the main [Effects screen](/docs/tour/effects), but filtered to show only effects belonging to this section.

## Right Column — Media & Navigation

### Related Media

A list of all media items associated with the area — videos, audio recordings, and photo galleries. Each item has a play button for quick access.

### Area Statistics

Summary counts for:
- 📹 Media items available
- 🎮 Interactive effects in this area
- 🔨 Construction time

### Navigation Links

Quick links back to the [Effects](/docs/tour/effects) screen, the [Media Library](/docs/tour/media), and the [Home](/docs/tour) page.
