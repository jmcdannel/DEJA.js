# Guest Effect Controls

The Effects screen lets tour guests interact with your layout's effects. Guests can trigger sounds, lighting changes, and animations through simple tap controls — no technical knowledge required.

## Effect Cards

Each guest-accessible effect appears as a card with a toggle button. Tap the card to activate the effect; tap again to deactivate it. Effects are organized in a responsive grid that works well on tablets and phones.

Effect types include:

- 💡 **Lighting** — Street lights, building lights, signal effects
- 🔊 **Sound** — Train horns, crossing bells, ambient audio
- 🎬 **Animation** — Moving elements, animated signs
- ⚡ **Relay** — Electrical switching for powered accessories

## Auto-Timeout Effects

Some effects automatically deactivate after a set duration — for example, a grade crossing bell might ring for 10 seconds before turning off, or a mountain echo horn might sound for 8 seconds. This prevents effects from being left on indefinitely by guests.

## Which Effects Appear

Only effects marked as **guest-accessible** in the [Cloud app](/docs/cloud/effects) appear on this screen. Layout operators control which effects visitors can interact with, keeping sensitive or operator-only effects hidden from the guest view.
