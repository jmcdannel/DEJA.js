# Your Locomotive Roster

The Roster screen displays all locomotives configured for your layout. From here, you can browse your collection and select a locomotive to drive.

## Viewing Your Roster

Each locomotive in the roster shows:

- **Name** — The locomotive's display name (e.g., "UP 4014 Big Boy")
- **DCC Address** — The decoder address used for communication
- **Road Name** — The railroad company (e.g., Union Pacific, BNSF)

Tap any locomotive to open its [throttle control screen](/docs/throttle/throttle) and start driving.

## Managing Your Roster

Locomotives are added and configured in the [Cloud app](/docs/cloud/roster). Changes made in the Cloud app — adding new locos, editing decoder functions, or building consists — appear in the Throttle roster automatically in real time.
