# Throttle Settings

The Settings screen lets you customize the Throttle app to suit your operating style.

## Connection Mode

Choose how the Throttle app communicates with your layout:

- **Firebase** — The default mode. Commands are sent through Firebase and picked up by the DEJA Server. Works from anywhere with an internet connection.
- **WiThrottle** — Connect directly to a WiThrottle-compatible command station on your local network. Lower latency, but requires a direct network connection.

## Theme

Switch between **Light** and **Dark** modes. The app defaults to your system preference.

## Footer Navigation

The bottom navigation bar shows your most-used pages as quick-access buttons. In Settings, you can customize which pages appear in the footer:

- Drag to reorder items
- Toggle items on or off
- Default favorites: Throttles, Conductor, Throttle, Effects, Routes, Turnouts, Signals

## Display Preferences

Additional display options let you fine-tune the Throttle experience for your setup and screen size.
