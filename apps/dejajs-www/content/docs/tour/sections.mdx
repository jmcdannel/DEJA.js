# Explore Sections

The Sections screen (`/sections`) presents the layout as a collection of themed areas. Each section represents a distinct part of the model railroad — a town, a mountain pass, a rail yard — with descriptions, photos, and related effects.

Tap a section to open its detail view with full media and interactive controls.
